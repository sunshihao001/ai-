This is a **B2 Finished Project Absorption Pack**. Its job is to understand a mature external project before the AI Method Wheel makes any `ADOPT`, `BRIDGE`, `MERGE`, `PATTERN_ONLY`, `WATCH`, or `REJECT` decision.
